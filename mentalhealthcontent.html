FAQ

Is this medical advice?

No. This is an educational content tool. It does not provide diagnosis or treatment and is not a substitute for professional care. Always include a crisis disclaimer and local resources when appropriate.

Is client data stored?

Avoid entering protected health information (PHI). We do not require PHI for content generation. Drafts you create can be deleted at any time from your account.

Each generation costs 1–5 credits depending on length and features (e.g., batch runs). Unused subscription credits roll over for 30 days.
